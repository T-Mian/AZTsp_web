Welcome to the website of our renowned company AZT Sp. z o. o., where the quality of training and customer satisfaction are our highest priorities. We have extensive experience in the welding industry, which allows us to provide services at the highest level. Our team of specialists consists of qualified instructors who have not only extensive theoretical knowledge, but also practical experience gained in various projects and industrial applications.



Our offer includes a variety of welding courses, adapted to different levels of experience and skills. For beginners, we offer basic training introducing the world of welding, covering theory, practice and issues related to occupational safety. For experienced welders, we offer advanced courses that expand their skills in specialized welding techniques, including TIG, MIG/MAG, electrode arc welding and gas welding.

Our instructors are not only well-prepared substantively, but also able to convey knowledge in a way that is accessible and understandable to participants. During training, we focus not only on theory, but also on practical aspects of welding, providing participants with the maximum amount of time for practical exercises and improving their skills under the supervision of experienced specialists.

An important element of our offer is the possibility of obtaining a TUV certificate, which is widely recognized and valued all over the world. Having this certificate confirms the high quality of training and professional competences of graduates, which can be extremely valuable for both employees and employers in the welding industry.



Our training includes not only theoretical knowledge, but also practical classes in our specially prepared workshops. Thanks to this, our participants have the opportunity to directly apply the acquired knowledge in practice, which allows them to quickly and effectively develop welding skills.

Our training program also focuses on occupational health and safety aspects. Participants in our courses are instructed in detail in the principles of safe welding and the use of the necessary protective equipment to ensure maximum safety while working.

Moreover, we also offer specialized courses that cover advanced welding techniques, such as controlled atmosphere welding and stainless steel welding. Thanks to this, our graduates are well prepared to work in various industry sectors where specialized welding skills are required.

Working with us means not only acquiring new skills, but also the opportunity for professional and career development. With a TUV certificate, our graduates can increase their attractiveness on the labor market and increase their chances of finding a satisfying job in the welding industry.

Our company, AZT Sp. z o. o., is distinguished not only by the excellent quality of training, but also by an individual approach to each participant. We understand that everyone has different goals and educational needs, which is why we try to adapt our courses to meet the expectations of both beginners who are just starting their adventure with welding and experienced professionals who want to improve their qualifications.

Basic welding courses

They include fundamental theoretical and practical knowledge, ideal for people starting their welding adventure. Participants acquire basic welding skills, including equipment operation, welding techniques and occupational health and safety principles.

Advanced welding courses

Aimed at people with some welding experience, these courses offer advanced welding techniques and methods such as TIG, MIG/MAG, stainless steel and aluminum welding. Participants have the opportunity to deepen their skills and expand their knowledge in specialized areas of welding.

Specialist courses

We also offer specialist courses that focus on specific welding areas and applications, such as controlled atmosphere welding, pipe welding or steel structure welding. These advanced courses are designed for people who want to gain specialized skills and knowledge in specific areas of welding.
